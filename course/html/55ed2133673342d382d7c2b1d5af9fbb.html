Explore: Funicular Forms

Use this simulation to experiment with loads, angles, and funicular forms. The Guiding Questions and Findings questions are designed to help you think about what to change and observe as you experiment.

Guiding Questions:

  • How does the height of the funicular form, h, affect the response? Design a funicular form and calculate the allowable load. Then adjust the height of the funicular form. If you increase the height what happens to the allowable load? If you decrease the height what happens to the allowable load?
  • How does the span of the funicular form, L, affect the response? Design a funicular form and and calculate the allowable load. Then adjust the span. If you increase the span what happens to the allowable load? If you decrease the span what happens to the allowable load?
  • How does the diameter of the cable affect the response? Design a funicular form and then change the diameter. What happens to the allowable load as you increase the diameter? decrease the diameter?
  • What is the relationship between the allowable load and tensile force in the cable? As one increases does the other also increase, decrease, or remain the same?
