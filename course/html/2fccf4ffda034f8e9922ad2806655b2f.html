Design Challenge: Cardboard Chair 

The challenge is to design a chair out of cardboard, duct tape and string. Use the design process to brainstorm, prototype and build a chair, which is really just a small-scale structure that must be able to support a load and be aesthetically pleasing. While we haven't discussed structural principles yet I hope this project will get you thinking about what types of structural systems may be used to resist loads, while applying the design process.
