Directions:
Each concept will include an Owl's Problem section where we ask you to look at what is happening and describe what you see. While we don't expect you to have the answers yet, you will likely have some thoughts about what is going on. Think for a few minutes and then type in your thoughts. Be creative!

The goal here is to get your thinking about the concepts we'll explore and to provide some context for the ideas.

What is Owl thinking? What could Owl be planning? How could Owl use engineering design to help solve the 'problem?'
