Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your cardboard table or design, we'd love to see it! And we encourage you to view and comment on each others' posts.

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build your cardboard table:

  • What was the inspiration for your design?
  • What part of your table resists tension? What part of table resists compression?
  • How did you support the table and why?
