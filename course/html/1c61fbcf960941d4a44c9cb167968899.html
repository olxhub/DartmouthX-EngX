Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your tensegrity sculpture, we'd love to see it! And we encourage you to view and comment on each others' posts.

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build your tensegrity sculptures:

  • How many dowels and elastic bands did you use to build your tensegrity structure(s)?
  • As you push on your tensegrity structure, what happens? You should find that tensegrity structures are fairly flexible, meaning that they can deform quite a bit. What are some of the advantages and disadvantages of a more flexible structure?
  • What was the inspiration for your design?
  • What part of the tensegrity structure resists tension? What part of the tensegrity structure resists compression?
