Illustrator: Katherine Roy

Directions:
Each concept will include an Owl's Problem section where we ask you to help us solve the 'problem' that Owl is facing.

Problem: Owl has too many books and needs to get them organized and easily accessible.

As a first step Owl plans to build a bridge over to a second tree (yes, Owl can fly but it is tough to fly while carrying heavy books). What type of bridge should Owl design? This week we'll focus on tension; what type of bridges could Owl design that rely on tension? What design might you propose? Let us know your thoughts.
