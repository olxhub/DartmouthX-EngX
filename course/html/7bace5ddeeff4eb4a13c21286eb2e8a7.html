Share Your World: Shear and Bending

Post images of beams and elements of structures that resist shear and bending.  Are there any in your local community you can share? As we go through the week, return back to these images and try to identify the flow of forces through the structure.

I used the following color coding for the arrows:

  • Red = compressive forces
  • Purple = tensile forces
  • Black = applied load
  • White = support forces
