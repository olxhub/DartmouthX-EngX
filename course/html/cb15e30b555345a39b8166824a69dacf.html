Share Your World: Compression and Tension Working Together (C4)

Post images of structures where tension and compression are working together but in separate elements. Look for trusses and tensegrity structures. Are there any in your local community you can share? As we go through the week, return back to these images and try to identify the flow of forces through the structure.

I used the following color coding for the arrows:

  • Red = compressive forces
  • Purple = tensile forces
  • Black = applied load
  • White = support forces
