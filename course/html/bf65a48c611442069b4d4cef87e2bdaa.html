Through Design Thinking we'll introduce you to a process used by engineers, architects and designers to create innovative solutions to problems and needs. This iterative design process will be used throughout the course to design and build. Also be sure to watch the guest lecture on sketching by David Macaulay, a MacArthur Fellow and the illustrator and author of many awarding winning books. We invite you to team up with friends, family, and colleagues for the Marshmallow Challenge. It's a quick activity and you can have some friendly competition over who can build the highest tower!
