Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your bridge design, we'd love to see it!

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build your cable-stayed bridge:

  • Tackle some of these questions in the discussion with your coursemates. Can you answer these questions for yourself by the end of your explorations?
  • What shape did you use for the tower(s)? Did you use a single tower or multiple towers? Where did you place your towers: to one side or spanning both sides?If you tried loading your bridge, how did you load it and how much load was it able to support?
  • How many cables did you use? Did you use a fan or harp design for your cables? Why?
  • What was the inspiration for you design?
  • If you could re-design your cable-stayed bridge, what would you change?
