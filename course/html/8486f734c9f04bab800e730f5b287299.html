Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your lateral force resisting structures, we'd love to see how you experimented! And we encourage you to view and comment on each others' posts.

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build and experiment with your lateral force resisting structures.

  • What did you use to build your lateral force resisting systems?
  • Which system is the stiffest, meaning that it deflects the least:  a moment-resisting frame, a  braced frame or a shearwall?
  • Which system is the most flexible, meaning that it deflects the most: a moment-resisting frame, a braced frame or a shearwall?
  • Which system is the strongest: a moment-resisting frame, a braced frame, or a shearwall?
