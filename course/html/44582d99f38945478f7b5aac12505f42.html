Share Your World: Tension (C2)

Post images of structures in tension from your community or around the world. As we go through the week, return back to these images and try to identify the flow of forces through the structure and where the structure might fail.

I used the following color coding for the arrows:

  • Red = compressive forces
  • Purple = tensile forces
  • Black = applied load
  • White = support forces

Come back and post images of funicular forms, bridges that use tension, and other structures where we see tension as we discuss them during Concept 2.
