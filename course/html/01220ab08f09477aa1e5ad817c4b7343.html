Lesson: Engineering and Architecture

A common question that I get from students is "What is the difference between engineering and architecture?" There is no easy answer to this question. In this video Karolina Kawiaka, an architect and lecturer here at Dartmouth, and Tim Eliassen, an engineer and the founder and president of TriPyramid Structures, Inc.  share a few of their thoughts on the differences and similarities between engineering and architecture and how engineers and architects collaborate on projects.
