Share Your World: Compression (C3)

Post images of structures in compression from your community or around the world. As we go through the week, return back to these images and try to identify the flow of forces through the structure and potential failure modes (buckling/compression).

I used the following color coding for the arrows:

  • Red = compressive forces
  • Purple = tensile forces
  • Black = applied load
  • White = support forces

Don't forget to complete the REPORT PARTICIPATION question.
