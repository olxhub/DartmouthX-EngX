The guidelines and estimated time-on-task below are suggestions to help you be successful. Have fun!

If you are challenging yourself to complete an Honor or Verified Certificate, please try our suggestions on the orange Certificate Track noting which sections are graded. Please reference the Certificate Requirements in the Syllabus and how many of each type of activity you need to participate in to get a passing grade.
