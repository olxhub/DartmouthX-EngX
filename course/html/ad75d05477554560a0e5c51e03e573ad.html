Share and Discuss

Share images of your sketches, prototypes/models, or final cardboard chair in the Design Challenge: Cardboard Chair (C1)  discussion found below!

Early next week we'll showcase some of the designs and chairs you created! Please use the voting feature in Discussion to vote up your favorites!

Additional questions you can choose to answer (pick one or two):

    • What worked with your design? What didn’t work with your design?
    • What was your design philosophy? Did you use a certain design as inspiration?
    • Does your chair include a back? Why or why not?
    • Is your chair comfortable? stable? aesthetically pleasing? You might want to have family and friends help evaluate these criteria as well.
    • What is innovative about your chair design?
    • Does your chair support your weight without too much deflection? 
    • What factors seemed to affect strength and stability of your chair?

Can't build? No worries! Share a sketch or description of your design!
