Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. We'd love to see how tall your column is and how much weight it can hold! Please share images and/or video and discuss your designs with peers.

Tackle some of these questions in the discussion for this activity:

  • How much weight did your paper columns support?
  • How tall were your paper columns? Show us a picture of your magnificent creation!
  • Did you use a single larger column or multiple smaller columns? Why?
  • What factors seem to affect how much load a column is able to support?
