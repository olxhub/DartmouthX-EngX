Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your antifunicular forms, we'd love to see it! We encourage you to view and comment on each others' posts.

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build your anti-funicular forms:

  • What was the inspiration for the anti-funicular form you chose?
  • What is creative about your design? Did you include holes in the cheesecloth? How many support points did you use?
  • What part of the anti-funicular form that you built resists compression?  tension?
  • How much load can your anti-funicular form support? How did you support the base when you loaded your anti-funicular form?
