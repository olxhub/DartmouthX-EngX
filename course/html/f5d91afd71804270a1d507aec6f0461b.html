Share and Discuss

Once you complete the design challenge – post pictures of your structure in the discussion area and reflect on the process by writing. Choose one or more of these guiding questions:

    • What was your design philosophy for your freestanding structure? Did you use all of the materials allowed?
    • What was innovative about your design?
    • How much time did you spend brainstorming and sketching? testing and building?
    • How tall was your structure? (include a numeric input so we can easily figure out the average height? and max height?)
    • How could you increase the height of your structure? If you could do the marshmallow challenge again, what would you do differently?
