Share and Discuss

Report your observations in the discussion. Don't forget to post pictures or a short video of your truss bridges. We'd love to see them! We encourage you to view and comment on each others' posts.

Here are a few questions we hope you'll consider as you design and build your truss bridges:

  • What type of truss did you design? why? how many truss members did you use?
  • How much load was your truss able to support? How did it fail?
  • How did you build the joints of your truss?
  • Which members of your truss do you expect to be in tension? compression?
  • How could you improve your design?
