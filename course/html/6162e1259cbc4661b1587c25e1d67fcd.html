Tutorial: Introduction to SketchUp Make

SketchUp (http://www.sketchup.com/) is modeling software that may be used to sketch, design and share models of structures. There is a free version (Make) that you can download to get started. While you are not required to use SketchUp in this course, I thought some of you might enjoy designing and sketching this way.

In this tutorial, Christopher Magoon, a design fellow here at the Thayer School of Engineering, steps through the design of a cardboard chair.
